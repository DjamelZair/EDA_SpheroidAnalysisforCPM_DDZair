Research context

CLL spheroids are 3-D tumour aggregates grown in 96-well plates and imaged continuously using the IncuCyte system. This pipeline audits image data quality, evaluates segmentation methods, links imaging findings to patient clinical metadata, and infers CPM energy parameters from spheroid-level morphological trajectories.
